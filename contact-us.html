Visit Our Farm

Our farm is located in the heart of Green Valley, just 15 minutes from downtown. We welcome visitors for educational means, class participation, and community needs.

Directions

From Highway 101, take a right on exit 23. Drive for 10 minutes down Farm-Fresh Avenue and take a left onto Green Valley Road. Drive until you see our Healing Kitchen sign and you have made it to your new favorite local farm.

Visiting Hours

Tours are by appointment only. Please contact us by phone or email to book a tour today. See class schedule for more information on classes that we offer.

How do I place an order for vegetables?

Send us an email or give us a call. If you are local, feel free to stop by and pick up whatever fresh produce you need, directly from our farm.

Do you offer delivery services?

We offer delivery services to anywhere in the US. Email or call to place an order.

Can I visit the farm with my family?

Yes! We encourage families to come together and learn about a good diet and sustainability. We believe it is of utmost importance to inform our community about the benefits of a healthy diet and fresh produce.
